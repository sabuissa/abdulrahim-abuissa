About

I'm a Qatari professional with a diverse background spanning corporate strategy, entrepreneurship, and public-sector engagement. Shaped by years of international education in the United States, I've built a career at the intersection of business, sport, and community development — grounded in clear communication, disciplined execution, and a strong commitment to long-term value creation.

As the first Qatari to compete at the NCAA Division I level, I've carried that competitive mindset into everything I do — from founding Abuissa Sports to advising on strategy at EY and representing Qatar as a FIBA Foundation Youth Leader.
