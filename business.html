Abuissa Sports

In January 2021, I founded Abuissa Sports — a youth-focused organization dedicated to athlete development, education, and character building, and home to one of Qatar's first Qatari-owned and operated basketball academies.

I lead its overall strategy and operations, from budgeting and recruitment to partnerships and program execution. We've brought NBA-standard coaching to young athletes through elite training camps, and built strong relationships with national institutions, international partners, and sports governing bodies along the way.

AI SPORTS Academy

AI SPORTS Academy is my latest venture — bringing together performance coaching, data analytics, and technology to create a new standard for athletic development in Qatar and the region.

The academy is built on the belief that the next generation of athletes will be shaped not just by physical training, but by how intelligently that training is designed, tracked, and refined. AI SPORTS Academy bridges the gap between elite coaching methodology and the tools of modern sports science.

Investments & Advisory

Beyond my own ventures, I invest in and advise early-stage companies and founders — particularly those working at the intersection of sports, technology, education, and community development.

I'm drawn to founders building things that have real-world impact, especially in markets like Qatar and the broader Gulf region where there is significant opportunity and growing demand for world-class products and services. If you're building something in these spaces and looking for a strategic partner, I'd love to hear from you.
